**Hero:**
"Frequently Asked Questions"

**Categories:**

### General Questions

**Q: What is debt settlement?**
A: Debt settlement is a process where we negotiate with your creditors to reduce the total amount you owe, often by 40-70%. You then pay the reduced amount in a lump sum or installments.

**Q: How long does the process take?**
A: Most settlements are completed within 24-48 months, depending on your debt amount and financial situation.

**Q: Is debt settlement legal in India?**
A: Yes, debt settlement is completely legal. We follow all RBI guidelines and ensure full legal compliance.

### Cost & Fees

**Q: How much do your services cost?**
A: We charge a success-based fee only after your debt is successfully settled. No upfront costs.

**Q: Are there any hidden charges?**
A: No. We believe in complete transparency. All fees are clearly explained before you sign up.

### Process Questions

**Q: Will this affect my credit score?**
A: Debt settlement may temporarily impact your credit score, but it's often better than bankruptcy or continued defaults.

**Q: Can I still use my credit cards during the process?**
A: We recommend stopping credit card usage during settlement to avoid accumulating more debt.

**Q: What if creditors don't agree to settle?**
A: Our expert negotiators have a 95% success rate. We work persistently to reach favorable settlements.

### Eligibility

**Q: Who is eligible for debt settlement?**
A: Anyone with unsecured debt (credit cards, personal loans) of ‚Çπ50,000 or more and facing financial hardship.

**Q: Do I need to be in default to qualify?**
A: No, but being in financial distress helps in negotiations.
